Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions we get the most. For a full list, visit our FAQ page.

How far in advance should I book?
↓
We recommend booking at least 3-4 months in advance for standard dates, and 6-8 months in advance for peak holiday seasons (Christmas, New Years) to secure your preferred villa type.
What is your cancellation policy?
↓
Cancellations made 30 days prior to check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 14-29 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 14 days of check-in are non-refundable.
Do you handle flights and visas?
↓
Currently, Nova Nest Retreats focuses exclusively on premium accommodation and ground transfers. However, our concierge desk can provide recommendations for trusted flight and visa booking partners.
Are taxes included in the displayed price?
↓
The base price shown on our resort cards does not include local government taxes and resort fees. A full breakdown of all taxes is provided on the booking page before you complete your reservation.
