About this resort

Experience the ultimate tropical escape at Azure Ocean Water Villas. Suspended above the crystal-clear waters of the Indian Ocean, our premium villas offer unmatched luxury, privacy, and panoramic views.

Each villa features a private infinity pool, glass-floor viewing panels, and direct lagoon access. Indulge in world-class dining at our underwater restaurant, rejuvenate at the holistic spa, or embark on a snorkeling adventure right from your deck.
