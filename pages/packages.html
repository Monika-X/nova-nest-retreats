Good To Know

Package FAQs

Quick answers before you choose a travel package.

Can I customize a listed package?

Yes. You can request upgrades, transfers, celebration extras, activity changes, or room category changes.

Are package prices per person?

Some packages are per person, while couple and family packages are priced for the stated group type.

Do packages include flights?

The listed packages focus on resort stays and inclusions. Flights can be handled separately if needed.

How do I book a large group?

Use the custom package CTA to request room blocks, group meals, meeting spaces, and shared transport.
