General Inquiries

What is Nova Nest Retreats?
+
Nova Nest Retreats is a premium booking platform specializing exclusively in luxury resorts and curated travel experiences worldwide. We partner with elite properties to offer our guests transparent pricing and exclusive perks.
How do I contact customer support?
+
Our concierge team is available 24/7. You can reach us via the Contact Us page, email us directly at support@novanest.com, or call our toll-free number 1-800-NOVANEST.
Do I need an account to make a booking?
+
No, you can book as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to access our Savings Calculator perks, manage your reservations easily, and participate in our Referral Rewards program.

Booking & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and selected regional payment gateways like UPI (in India) and Apple Pay.
Can I pay for my booking in installments?
+
Yes! For bookings made more than 60 days in advance, we offer a "Pay Later" option where you can pay a 25% deposit to secure your booking, and pay the remaining balance 30 days before check-in.
Are taxes included in the displayed price?
+
The base prices shown on the resort cards do not include local taxes and resort fees. A full breakdown of all taxes is explicitly provided on the checkout page before you complete your reservation.

Cancellations & Modifications

What is your standard cancellation policy?
+
Cancellations made 30 days prior to check-in receive a full refund. Cancellations made within 14-29 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 14 days of check-in are non-refundable. Note: Flash Sale and special promotional packages may be entirely non-refundable.
How do I modify my travel dates?
+
You can request date modifications through the "My Bookings" section of your User Dashboard. Modifications are subject to resort availability and may incur a rate difference if the new dates fall in a different pricing season.

Resort Facilities & Packages

Do you handle flights and visas?
+
Nova Nest Retreats focuses exclusively on premium accommodation and ground/water transfers. However, our concierge desk can provide recommendations for trusted flight and visa booking partners.
Are pets allowed at the resorts?
+
Pet policies vary heavily by property. You can use the "Pet Friendly" filter on the Resorts page to find accommodating locations. Please note that most international island resorts (like the Maldives) have strict no-pet policies due to local ecology laws.
