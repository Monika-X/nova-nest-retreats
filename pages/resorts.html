Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for browsing, filtering, and choosing a resort.

How do I find budget resorts?

Use the max budget slider, then sort by price low to high to bring affordable stays to the top.

Can I search by destination?

Yes. Type a city, country, or resort name in the search field and the grid updates automatically.

What does the category filter do?

It narrows the list to resort styles like beachfront, jungle, mountain, city luxury, or private island.

Where do saved resorts go?

Wishlist selections are stored in your browser so you can return to your favorite options during planning.
