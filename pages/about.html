Our Story

Born From a Passion for Paradise

Founded in 2015, Nova Nest Retreats started with a simple observation: finding truly exceptional luxury resorts shouldn't be an exhausting process. Our founders spent years traversing the globe, identifying properties that didn't just offer a bed, but an experience that stirs the soul.

Today, we are a leading bespoke travel platform. We exclusively partner with resorts that meet our rigorous standards for hospitality, aesthetic brilliance, and environmental sustainability. When you book with Nova Nest, you aren't just booking a room—you're reserving a memory.
