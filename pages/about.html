Our Story

Born From a Passion for Paradise

Founded in 2015, Nova Nest Retreats started with a simple observation: finding truly exceptional luxury resorts shouldn't be exhausting. Our founders spent years traversing the globe, identifying properties that stir the soul.

Today, we are a leading bespoke travel platform exclusively partnering with resorts that meet our rigorous standards for hospitality, aesthetic brilliance, and environmental sustainability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nova Nest a resort chain?

No. Nova Nest is a curated travel platform that connects guests with premium resort partners.

How are resorts selected?

We look at location, service, comfort, amenities, guest value, and clarity of information.

Can you help with special trips?

Yes. Guests can request help for celebrations, groups, workations, family trips, and custom packages.

Do you support budget travel?

Yes. We include budget recommendations and packages that balance comfort with practical pricing.
