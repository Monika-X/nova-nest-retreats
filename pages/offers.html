Offer Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you apply a deal or coupon.

Can I use two coupon codes together?

Most offers allow one promo code per booking, though member benefits or bank perks may apply separately when eligible.

Do flash sale prices change?

Yes. Flash sale inventory can change as rooms sell out or the countdown ends.

Are offers valid on every resort?

Some offers are limited to selected resorts, room categories, destinations, or travel dates.

Where do I apply a bank offer?

Bank offers are typically applied at payment after choosing an eligible card or payment method.
