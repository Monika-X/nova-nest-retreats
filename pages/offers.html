Common Questions

Offer FAQs

Quick answers about how our deals and discounts work.

Can I combine multiple promo codes?

No, only one promo code can be applied per booking. We recommend choosing the code with the highest discount for your booking value.

Do member discounts stack with seasonal offers?

In most cases, yes. Member tier discounts can be applied on top of seasonal offers, unless the deal is explicitly marked "not stackable."

Are flash sale bookings refundable?

Flash sale bookings are generally non-refundable but can be rescheduled once within the same season, subject to availability.

How long are coupon codes valid?

Each coupon has a validity window shown on its card. Expired codes cannot be applied. Subscribe to get alerts before deals expire.
