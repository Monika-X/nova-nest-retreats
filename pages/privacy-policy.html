1. Company Policy & Privacy Commitments

At Nova Nest Retreats, we believe that luxury is built on trust. Our core company policy is centered around maintaining the highest standards of confidentiality, data integrity, and digital security. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors, registered members, and booking guests.

Our Core Principles

  • Transparency: We will always detail what credentials we compile and exactly why they are processed.
  • Minimalism: We only request data variables that are essential to secure your resort stays.
  • Exclusivity: Your personal travel choices remain yours. We do not sell or trade user list metrics to third-party advertisers.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We collect and utilize user parameters in order to deliver premium holiday packages, secure payment structures, and customize stays at our partner resorts.

Types of Data Collected

  • Personal Details: Full name, date of birth, identity proofs (if required by resort regulations).
  • Contact Information: Active email, phone number, and physical billing addresses.
  • Booking Logs: Travel dates, selected resort names, room configuration choices, and guest preference lists.
  • Financial Logs: Transaction values, receipt numbers, and payment status indicators.

How We Use Your Data

All processed information is used strictly under legal frameworks for:

  • Completing and managing your resort stay bookings.
  • Updating check-in, billing status, or custom discount rewards.
  • Verifying membership status levels (e.g. Gold Member level privileges).
  • Improving platform navigability based on diagnostic analytics.

3. User Protection & Rights

We incorporate comprehensive protective controls to insulate your credentials from unauthorized access, loss, or leakage.

Encryption & Infrastructure Security

All user portals, dashboards, and booking forms utilize Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption to defend data in transit. Databases are shielded behind advanced corporate firewalls and checked for vulnerability issues regularly.

Your Rights & Controls

As a valued user, you have absolute control over your personal records:

  • Access & Correction: You can modify your name, email, and preferences anytime in your account settings.
  • Data Portability: You may request a downloadable summary of your booking history.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You have the right to request the complete deletion of your account and historical data, subject to local tax or legal auditing mandates.
  • Opt-Out Options: You can toggle promotional email updates off instantly in your settings panel.

4. Cookies & Tracking Policy

Our website uses cookies (small text files saved on your computer or device) to enhance system loading times, retain active user sessions, and customize interfaces.

Categories of Cookies Used

  • Essential Cookies: Crucial to enable login status, booking selection steps, and checkout payment portals.
  • Preferences Cookies: Retain choices like preferred language, dark mode theme styles, and active filter preferences.
  • Wishlist & Save Cookies: Save your favorite resort properties temporarily so you can access them again later.
  • Analytical Cookies: Compile anonymous traffic counts and visitor journeys to help us optimize page speed and design layout.

How to Control Cookies

Most modern web browsers allow you to block or delete cookies entirely via browser configuration settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact certain interactive features, such as checking booking statuses or editing settings.
